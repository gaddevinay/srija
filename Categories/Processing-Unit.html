Efficient & Reliable Processing Units

Overview

Our processing unit is located in Hyderabad (Raj Bollaram). Here, the seeds are carefully treated and packed after further filtration processes done by machines and handpicking for outlet into the seed market. This ensures we provide farmers with high-quality hybrid seeds.

The seed processing follows a unique process. First, the seeds are dried in a dryer to remove any moisture. Then, the seeds are transferred to processing machines. The processing machine consists of a set of 6 different machines, each functioning uniquely for a specific purpose.
