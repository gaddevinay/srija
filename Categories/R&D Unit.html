Research & Development

We develope best seed here

OUR R&D UNIT IS LOCATED IN HYDERABAD(MEDCHAL) WHICH IS SPREAD OVER AN AREA OF 10 ACRES, HERE OUR QUALIFIED SCIENTISTS WORK ON SEEDS. THEY TRY TO IMPROVE THE NUTRITIONAL VALUES AND DISEASE RESISTANCY AND OTHER CHARECTERS OF SEEDS BY CROSSING THEM WITH WILD PANTS WITH THE DESIRED CHARECTERS, FINALLY THE DESIRED CHARECTERS GETS EMBEDED IN THE BABY SEED.
LATER ON THESE BABY SEEDS(FOUNDATION) UNDERGO FURTHER TESTS SUCH AS GERMINATION TEST, MOISTURE TEST ETC.. UNDER LABORATORIC CONDITIONS AND IF THE SEEDS PASS OUT THESE TESTS THEY ARE SOWED IN NATURAL CLIMATE FOR A RE-TEST OF THEIR PERFORMANCE IN OUTER LABORATORIC CONDITIONS. AFTER ANALYSING THE SEEDS ARE SENT FOR FURTHER TESTS AT AGRICULTURAL UNIVERSITIES, THEN AFTER ACHIEVING A PASS OUT RESULT WE SEND THE SEED FOR PRODUCTION AT OUR PRODUCTION HOUSES.
